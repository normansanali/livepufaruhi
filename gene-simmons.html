What is Gene Simmonss Net Worth: Biography, Net Worth &amp; more

Gene Simmons is a $400 million net-worth American rock & roll legend. Gene Simmons is best known as a co-founder of the band Kiss, which was immensely popular in the late 1970s and early 1990s, selling over 100 million records worldwide as of this writing. As a member of Kiss, Simmons was inducted into the Rock & Roll Hall of Fame in 2014.

Who is Gene Simmons?

Gene Simmons was born Chaim Witz in Haifa, Israel on August 25, 1949. He was born to Florence Klein and carpenter Ferenc Yehiel Witz. During the Holocaust, his mother and her brother, Gene’s uncle Larry Klein, were the only members of their family to survive imprisonment in a Nazi concentration camp. Gene relocated to Jackson Heights, Queens, New York City, with his mother when he was eight years old. Chaim Witz changed his name to Eugene Klein after moving to America.

After seeing The Beatles on the Ed Sullivan Program, Johnny got tremendously interested in music. With his newfound musical passion, his mother bought him a used guitar. Gene grew proficient on the guitar but chose to switch to bass since he believed it would be simpler to get into a band. Eugene formed his first band, Lynx, in high school, which was eventually renamed Missing Links. Simmons joined The Long Island Sounds after leaving The Missing Links.

Gene attended Sullivan County Community College for a short time before transferring to Richmond College, where he earned a degree in education. Simmons used his degree to teach grade school in Spanish Harlem and even work as a personal assistant to the editors of “Vogue” and “Glamour” magazines. During this time, he changed his name to Gene Simmons in honor of rockabilly legend “Jumpin’ Gene Simmons.” Simmons formed a new band, Bullfrog Bheer, and the demo Leeta was recorded. (It was eventually released as part of the Kiss box set.)
