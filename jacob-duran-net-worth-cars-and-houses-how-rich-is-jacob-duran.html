Jacob Duran Net Worth, Cars and Houses: How rich is Jacob Duran?

A professional cutman for boxing and mixed martial arts matches, Jacob “Stitch” Duran was born on December 29, 1951. Being raised in Planada, California, he is of Mexican origin.

A former owner and manager in Fairfield, California, Duran maintained a boxing and kickboxing facility. In addition, he was one of the earliest IKF promoters in California, organizing his first event in October 1992. Prior to pursuing a professional boxing and mixed martial arts career, Duran trained and produced a number of the first IKF Amateur Champions, including Randy “Buzzman” Bussart, Mike “Mohamid” Duran, John “Psyco” Parker, and Andy “The Sandman” Sanchez. In the recent past, Duran has worked with Tyson Fury as well as the Klitschko brothers, Andre Ward, and others.

Jacob Duran Net Worth, Cars and Houses: How rich is Jacob Duran?

The estimated net worth of Jacob Duran as of 2021 is $5 million. His employment as a professional cutman is his main source of income.

In addition, he has acted in a number of films, such as Creed, Here Comes the Boom, Rocky Balboa, Undisputed, Ocean’s Eleven, and Play it to the Bone, which has helped him significantly increase his net worth.
