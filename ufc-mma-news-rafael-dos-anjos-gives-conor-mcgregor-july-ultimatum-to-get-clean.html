Rafael dos Anjos Gives Conor McGregor July Ultimatum to Get &quot;clean&quot;

Dos Anjos after emerging victorious called out the Irishman to settle old business with him. He said, “Conor McGregor, seven years ago I earned this fight. We were supposed to fight, I broke my foot. He has a couple of months to clean his body of all the stuff he is taking. Here I am. I will see you in July.”
