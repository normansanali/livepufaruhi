Biography

Umaga was born in San Francisco, CA on March 28, 1973. Known as “The Samoan Savage,” he was a two-time International Champion in the WWE, and part of the tag team known as the Samoan Gangstas and 3-Minute Warning. He was matched up with Donald Trump at WrestleMania 23 Battle of the Billionaires, where he wrestled against Jeff Hardy. After training at the Wild Samoan Pro Wrestling Training Center, run by his uncles Afa and Sika, he began wrestling for Afa’s World Xtreme Wrestling promotion.
