Meet Isha Lopez! Bio, Age, Career, Husband

There’s a new TLC show that we think you people might like. Bad Hair Day is a makeover show that follows patients with extreme hair-related medical issues as they meet with expert doctors, namely Dr. Angela Phipps, Dr. Meena Singh, and Dr. Isha Lopez, for life-changing procedures and treatments.

Dr. Isha is a dermatologist and can be seen helping patients with their hair-related issues in the show, Bad Hair Day. Learn everything about her in the wiki article below.

Isha Lopez On TLC’s Bad Hair Day

Dr. Isha had more than 11 years of experience in the medical field coming into the TLC show Bad Hair Day. Her specialties include diagnosing and treating skin diseases and performing cosmetic procedures, including hair removal.

As per the press release, Isha has seen patients including “a woman who is self-conscious about her psoriasis, a man who uses his hair to hide a large growth on his head, and a couple who believes that one of them suffers from ‘smelly hair syndrome'” in her career.

Isha first shared about her TV project, Bad Hair Day, to her fans and followers on August 15, 2022, via an Instagram post. She shared a promotional video of the show and asked her followers to join her on this amazing journey.

In her chat with Houston Life on August 31st, Isha shared how she got tapped for the show. She revealed that an email had come to her office from the show asking for a dermatologist. Isha’s founder, Kim, suggested she should join the show since she was a woman and a Hispanic.

Isha Lopez Career

Isha Lopez is a popular dermatologist from Houston, Texas. She currently practices dermatology at Memorial Hermann Pearland Hospital and Memorial Hermann Southeast Hospital. She has served in several cities of Texas, including Channelview, Deer Park, Friendswood, Galena Park, Houston, La Porte, League City, Pasadena, Pearland, Seabrook, South Houston, and Webster.

According to health.usnews.com, Isha’s specialties include diagnosing and treating skin-related diseases and performing cosmetic procedures like hair removal, laser therapy, cosmetic filler injections, cryosurgery, tattoo removal, and phototherapy.

In her office, Dr. Isha usually deals with patients who need follicular fixes. She is also proficient in other medical domains such as Tinea Unguium, Sunburn, Athlete’s Foot, Varicose Veins, Plantar Warts, and Rosacea.

Isha graduated from the University of Texas Southwestern Medical School. She interned at Baylor College of Medicine and the University of Texas Southwestern Medical Center. Isha later went on to complete her residency at the University of Texas Health System.

Isha studied Biochemistry and Molecular Biology at Houston Baptist University, according to her Facebook. She also went to Northshore High School.
