Tyanna Wallace Mother: Who Is Jan Jackson?

Through her father Notorious B.I.G., she is linked to Voletta Wallace and George Latore.
T’yanna made a cameo in the movie Biggie: I Got A Story To Tell.

T’yanna is a fashion designer and owns a boutique store. Notorious B.I.G., her father, was largely regarded as one of the best rappers to ever live. His songs frequently had a semi-autobiographical tone and spoke of adversity and crime as well as revelry and happiness.
Biggie passed away on March 9, 1997, in Los Angeles, California, at the age of 24.
