L-J Cleave - Age, Family, Bio

TikTok creator who has more than 2.5 million fans on her account. She is known for posting original comedy videos about being Scottish and for portraying her two characters named Margret and Linda. She also posts vlogs, makeup tutorials, and TikTok tips to her self-titled YouTube channel. 

Before Fame

While studying musical theatre at Fife College, she worked as a care assistant for adults and children with learning disabilities. 

Trivia

An actress as well a social star, she appeared as Linda in the films Lord of Tears and The Owlman.

Family Life

She is close with her grandmother, Heather, and her younger brother, fellow social star Robin Kane. She began a relationship with her collaborator Alex VanCleave, who later proposed to her in May of 2018. 

Associated With

She often collaborates with fellow social star Bella Rose Dunn.
