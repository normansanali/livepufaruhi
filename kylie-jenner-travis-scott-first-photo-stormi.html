Kylie Jenner and Travis Scott Take First Photo Together Since Daughter Stormis Birth

It's not surprising that there aren't many photos of Kylie Jenner and Travis Scott together. The couple just welcomed their first child together, baby Stormi, after managing to keep Jenner's entire pregnancy completely out of the spotlight. Basically, they're masters when it comes to hiding out from paparazzi and eager fans alike.

However, a rare picture of the couple appeared on Scott's Snapchat account late Thursday night. In the slightly strange shot, Scott and Jenner both don face masks. But clearly they're having a lot of fun together as they raise their newborn baby:

Snapchat

The significance of the medical face masks is unclear, but as both Scott and Jenner are so skilled at hiding from the public eye, it's possible that this is just another disguise that the couple uses.

Jenner and Scott were first seen together in April 2017 at a Coachella party. Later that month they were spotted courtside at an NBA playoff game in Houston.

The first photo of the couple's baby, Stormi, currently has over 17 million likes on Instagram:

Jenner announced Stormi's birth on February 4, 2018, after managing to keep her pregnancy a secret, despite rumors breaking in September 2017. She also shared an emotional video on social media, which documented her entire journey to becoming a new mother. The intimate documentary showcased Jenner's growing baby bump, and the family's reactions to the baby news.

Of her pregnancy, Jenner wrote on social media, "Pregnancy has been the most beautiful, empowering, and life changing experience I’ve had in my entire life and I'm actually going to miss it. I appreciate my friends and especially my family for helping me make this special moment as private as we could.”
