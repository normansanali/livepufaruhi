Jax Dane And More Announced For Velvet After Dark Edition Of NWA Powerrr On 10/25

Velvet Sky will host a special episode of NWA Powerrr on October 25.

Titled “Velvet After Dark”, the “steamy” show promises to feature plenty of action. Former National Champion Jax Dane will compete in a singles match against Sal Rinauro. Plus, Rolando Freeman will take on KC Roxx, and Traxx will battle Anthony Andrews. Elsewhere on the show, Sodapop Hendrix will clash with Eric Jackson.

The card is as follows:

  • Jax Dane vs. Sal Rinauro
  • Rolando Freeman vs. KC Roxx
  • Traxx vs. Anthony Andrews
  • Sodapop Hendrix vs. Eric Jackson
