Head in the Clouds festival reveals 2021 lineup, with Saweetie, Joji and more coming to Pasadena

Saweetie, Joji, Rich Brian and Niki top the 2021 lineup for the Head In The Clouds Music & Arts Festival, which will bring more than two dozen artists to Brookside, the golf course adjacent to Pasadena’s Rose Bowl, on Saturday, Nov. 6, and Sunday, Nov. 7.

Tickets for the Asian hip-hop, R&B, pop, and dance music-oriented event go on sale at noon on Thursday, July 29, to see Japanese-Australian R&B singer Joji, Indonesian rapper Rich Brian, Indonesian singer-songwriter Niki, rapper Saweetie, who recently released as song titled “Best Friend” with Doja Cat, as well as indie rockers Japanese Breakfast.

The festival, which is produced by Asian music and media company 88rising and Southern California concert promoter Goldenvoice, has grown from 10,000 people attending in 2018 and more than 22,000 the following year at  the Los Angeles State Historic Park.

General admission passes are $249, excluding taxes and fees, VIP passes are $399, excluding taxes and fees.

For tickets and more information, visit 88rising.com.
