Sissy Spacek Net Worth

Sissy Spacek is an American actress, singer, and author with a net worth of $15 million.

Spacek’s memorable roles include Carrie White in “Carrie” (1976), Loretta Lynn in “Coal Miner’s Daughter” (1980), and Sally Rayburn in the Netflix series “Bloodline” (2015–2017).

Spacek has also dabbled in music, recording vocals for the Coal Miner’s Daughter soundtrack album.

The album peaked at number two on the Billboard Top Country Albums Chart and earned her a Grammy nomination for Best Female Country Vocal Performance.

Hangin’ Up My Heart (1983), her studio album, was critically acclaimed and peaked at number 17 on the Billboard Top Country Albums chart.

Sissy Spacek was nominated for a Primetime Emmy Award for her roles in the television films The Good Old Boys (1995) and Last Call (2002), as well as her appearance on the HBO drama series Big Love (2011).

She played Sally Rayburn, the matriarch, on the Netflix drama thriller series Bloodline (2015-2017), Ruth Deaver on the Hulu psychological horror series Castle Rock (2018), and Ellen Bergman on the Amazon Prime Video psychological thriller series Homecoming (2018).
