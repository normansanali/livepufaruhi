Who Was Dani Hampson? Her Bio, Age, Job, Parents

Losing someone special is heartbreaking, and even more when it’s on a special day. X Factor performer, Tom Mann’s fiancee Dani Hampson suddenly died on the morning of their wedding, leaving the singer in an “ocean” of tears and grief-stricken.

So, who was she? Get to know all about Tom’s late fiancee as this Dani Hampson Bio proceeds. 

Meet Dani Hampson, Tom Mann Fiancee

Dani Hampson and Tom Mann were together ever since 2009. “You took me under your wing & have had my back ever since,” Tom recalled his late fiancee.

Fast forward to May 2019, the lovebirds then bought their first home they’d like to call “our diamond in the rough and our safe place.” Also, in December that very year, the singer popped the question to the love of his life with a beautiful diamond ring at the Piano Bar and Restaurant in Piano Kensington.

“Christmas came early. Introducing my future husband,” Dani shared her happiness at the time v.i.a her IG.

So, with everything in place, Dani and Tom then planned to get married in 2020. But sadly, COVID hit the world and everything went on lockdown.

Likewise, their plans to get married in 2021 also got postponed due to their professional lives. However, amidst disappointments, on October 18, 2021, the duo shared the good news that their baby Bowie Andrew Mann — their “highlight, proudest moment & biggest achievement” — stepped into his world.

“We are saturated with a love I didn’t know existed & one I will never be able to put into words. Bowie Andrew Mann Born 18.10.2021. You have changed our lives forever,” Dani shared over an IG post feating their little one at the time.

Also, starting the year 2022, Dani recalled, “2021 started much like 2020 – expecting to become husband & wife – only we ended up as mum & dad instead.”

But little did the star performer know that 2022 was going to be the darkest year of his life. On June 18, 2022, the day he was supposed to officially become Dani’s husband, she left this world.

Dani Hampson Cause Of Death

It was Tom Mann who first revealed the news about his fiancee’s death v.i.a an IG post, but he didn’t mention her cause of death. However, as per “celebseek.com,” Dani Hampson had just returned from a vacation in Sardinia with Tom and she had no known medical issues at the time. So, her death’s certainly a mystery.

“I can’t believe I am writing these words but my darling Dani – my best friend, my everything and more, the love of my life – passed away in the early hours of Saturday morning, 18th June,”  Tom wrote over his IG.

He then followed the paragraph with long words of love grieving and recalling all their days in the sun. To summarize, Tom might not have had the chance to marry his fiancee, but she was already his entire world. He promised to wear the ring he was supposed to as a sign of his unconditional love.

Also, Tom added that though he was lost without, he will be strong for the sake of their little one. “I will not be a mark on the parent that you had already become but I promise I will do my everything to raise Bowie just the way we always wanted,” he promised.

Dani Hampson Age At The Time Of Her Death

Dani Hampson was 34 years of age at the time of her death.

She was 6 years older than her fiance Tom.

Talking about their age gap, Dani once joked, “The thing about our age gap is he’s like a fine wine & I’m aging at the speed of unrefrigerated milk.”

Fun Fact: Tom sang Elton John’s “Rocket Man” almost every day of Dani’s 3rd trimester. The son was also in Dani’s labor playlist and was number 1 the day Bowie was born.

What Do We Know About Dani Hampson Parents?

Dani Hampson was born to parents, Martin and Janette Hampson. She and Tom were very close to them and they’ve also been on a few vacations together. Infact, her parents would also come to stay at Dani and Tom’s place during their travel to London.

As for Dani’s siblings, she has a 3-year-younger brother named Andrew Hampson (birthday: Feb 25). In 2022, he resided in  Manchester, the United Kingdom with his girlfriend Helena Smith.

Also, Dani had a loving grandfather named Bernard who was born on December 10, 1929. The last we saw him, he was alive and healthy.

Besides dancing, Dani also worked as a PR executive at Becky Knowles Communications. She started her job there exactly a year after her internship.
