Trae Young Parents: Everything You Need to Know About the NBA Stars Family

Trae Young is one of the most exciting and talented young players in the NBA.

The Atlanta Hawks point guard has dazzled fans with his scoring, passing, and clutch performances.

But who are the people behind his success? Here is everything you need to know about Trae Young’s parents and family.

Trae Young Age

Trae Young was born on September 19, 1998, in Lubbock, Texas.

He is currently 24 years old as of September 2023.

He grew up in Norman, Oklahoma, where he attended Norman North High School and starred as a basketball player.

Trae Young Parents

Trae Young is the son of Candice and Rayford Young, who both attended Texas Tech University.

His father, Rayford, played basketball for the Texas Tech Red Raiders from 1996 to 2000.

He was a prolific scorer who once dropped 41 points against Kansas in his senior year.

He also played professionally in Europe for several years before becoming an assistant coach at Norman North High School.

His mother, Candice, is a former cheerleader who met Rayford at Pampa High School in Texas.

They got married in 1997 and have four children: Trae, Timothy, Camryn, and Caitlyn.

Trae Young Net Worth

Trae Young’s net worth is estimated to be around $55 million as of September 2023.

His main source of income is his basketball salary from the Atlanta Hawks, which will increase significantly from the next season due to his contract extension.

He also earns money from endorsements and sponsorships from various brands such as Adidas, BodyArmor, Panini America, Express Men’s Clothing, State Farm Insurance, Beats by Dre headphones, and Chipotle Mexican Grill.

He also has his own foundation called the Trae Young Family Foundation, which supports mental health awareness and youth development in his hometown of Norman, Oklahoma.

Trae Young Height

Young is listed at 6 feet 1 inch (1.85 meters) tall.

Trae Young weight is around 180 pounds (82 kilograms).

He is considered relatively small for an NBA player, especially for his position as a point guard.

However, he makes up for his lack of size with his speed, agility, skill, and creativity.

He is also known for his long-range shooting ability and his signature floater shot.

Trae Young Wife

Trae Young married his longtime girlfriend Shelby Miller on July 22, 2023, in a lavish ceremony in the Bahamas.

The couple met at the University of Oklahoma, where Miller was a cheerleader and Young was a basketball star.

They started dating in 2017 and got engaged in December 2021.

The two have one son together, Tydus Reign Young, who was born on June 2, 2022.

They are also expecting a baby girl soon.

Trae Young Contract

Trae Young signed a four-year rookie contract with the Atlanta Hawks in 2018, worth $26.5 million.

He earned $6.3 million in his first season, $6.6 million in his second season, $6.9 million in his third season, and $8.3 million in his fourth season.

In August 2021, he agreed to a five-year contract extension with the Hawks, worth $207 million.

The extension will kick in from the 2022-23 season and will make him one of the highest-paid players in the league.
