Hoda Kotb Shares Portrait with Family, Who Showed Strong Resemblance to Her, on Brother's Birthday

Hoda Kotb shared a heartwarming family portrait with fans, showing off their striking resemblance. Fans couldn't help but gush about the anchor's beautiful family.

Hoda Kotb has always been vocal about her close bond with her family. She shared another family portrait on Instagram, showing their striking resemblance and their heartwarming tight-knit bond.

Hoda shared the new family photo in celebration of her brother Adel's birthday. The family gathered around a couch and were on the brink of laughter when someone took the picture.

"Family is everything! Your smile says it all!" one of her followers said. "What a great photo filled with happiness and love," another echoed.

Hoda, who has a brother and sister, remains close to her siblings. In 2021, the TV host revealed that at one of her breaking points, her brother Adel was there to help her and motivate her to carry on.

While running the Boston Marathon, Hoda could feel herself giving up in the last six miles. Her mind was already conditioning her to accept that she wouldn't make it, but her brother wouldn't allow it.

With his street shoes, Adel jumped in and ran the final stretch of the marathon with Hoda. Hoda's friend and co-host, Jenna Bush-Hager, asked whether it was because she asked him to or because he could read it in her eyes.

"He just read it," Hoda said. "We crossed the finish line together." Adel finished the final 6 miles of the Boston Marathon in loafers.

The Kotb family has been supportive of Hoda's endeavors through the years. Through all her highs and lows, they've made sure to be there for the TV host and her two daughters.
