Mayweather’s Boxing Career Earnings

Floyd Mayweather’s boxing career has been the primary source of his wealth. Known for his strategic fighting style and defensive prowess, Mayweather has capitalized on high-profile bouts that have generated substantial pay-per-view revenues. Let’s break down how his boxing career has contributed to his net worth:
