Daria Kasatkina family, parents, siblings, gay partner, children

The iconic Daria Sergeyevna Kasatkina is a Russian professional tennis player. who is currently the Russian No. 1 player in singles She made her top-ten debut in the WTA rankings towards the end of 2018.

She has won four WTA Tour titles in singles as well as one title in doubles. Kasatkina started playing tennis at the age of six at the insistence of her older brother. She was born to competitive parents who were nationally ranked in ice hockey and athletics.
