Meet Claim To Fame L.C.! Bio, Age, Instagram, Famous Relative

L.C. of Claim To Fame is the acronym for one of the contestants of the show who viewers believe is someone related to KeKe Palmer. But is the speculation true? Learn more about who L.C. is in this article. Learn about her age, Instagram, and famous relatives in this piece.

This article covers it all below from here onwards.

Meet L.C., One Of The Contestants On Claim To Fame

Kevin and Frankie Jonas, the oldest and youngest of the Jonas Brothers, respectively, host the ABC Program Claim To Fame. The final person standing will get a prize of $100,000. All 12 candidates are connected to famous people and compete by trying to guess each other’s, famous relatives. Every week, a contender must guess the identity of another participant, and either they or the guessee are eliminated.

In the first episode of Claim To Fame, L.C. sang the off-key version of Francis Scott’s The Star-Spangled Banner. In the confessional, she says, “There’s a reason we’re the relative and not the celebrity”. She lands in the bottom two in the talent show challenge with Pepper.

She almost became the guesser but Pepper was voted as the guesser in the first as well as the second episode.

So, far two contestants are out. In a surprise turn of events, Maxwell, Chuck Norris’ grandson, was ousted from the tournament in the first episode. Participants chose Pepper to be the first guesser. She decided on Maxwell and was certain he was Steven Spielberg’s descendant. When Maxwell used a smartphone to cheat during the game, hosts Joe and Frankie Jonas were briefly removed from the camera.

Because it was against the rules, he was disqualified and sent home. However, Maxwell cheated, protecting Pepper from being removed. For episode 2, Pepper was once more chosen as the guesser. She made the choice of Michael and was right to assume that Michael was linked to Zendaya. In reality, Zendaya’s cousin is Michael.

Claim To Fame L.C. Full Name

The full name of Claim To Fame L.C. is Loreal Chanel Wimberly.

Who Is Claim To Fame L.C. Famous Relative?

In the show, L.C. like all the contestants shared four clues. The first clue was her famous relative was her father which is a lie. Then, she states that her famous relative is an actor and has won an Emmy. But, sleuths on the internet were ready to work with that. L.C. has been elected as a guessee yet.

Some eagle-eyed fans noticed that L.C. bears a striking resemblance to actress Keke Palmer who just won an Emmy in 2021. A Twitter user named (@LIVtheVIRGO) posted a picture of alleged L.C. with KeKe.

Loreal is the firstborn of Sharon Palmer and Larry Palmer.

Sharon is the co-founder/manager/management staff of Watch Out For Keke for 22 years. She co-founded Saving Our Cinderella. Her father attended St. Joseph’s grade school in Cabrini Green. He also attended Northern Illinois University.

Three siblings exist for Keke Palmer. Twins Lawrence and Lawrencia Palmer are the youngest two. Lawrencia is a graduate of Florida A&M University. Meanwhile, Lawrence graduated from Pasadena High School.

Lawrencia’s Instagram account clearly shows that she is not L.C. A tagged image on Lawrencia’s profile led us to Keke’s older sister’s Instagram account. Keke’s older sister Loreal or “LoLo ChaCha” has a similar appearance to L.C. on her Instagram profile photo, despite the fact that it is private.

@lorealchanelll, which, lo and behold, may be “L.C.” for short, is Loreal’s Instagram handle.

Another item to note is that Loreal’s profile image shows a tattoo of a peace sign on her left wrist. Despite our best efforts, we were unable to see L.C.’s left wrist throughout Episode 1 because of the practicality of her long sleeves.

While some viewers are noting her resemblance to Keke Palmer, L.C. won over the crowd when she joked about being “5.679% Whitney [Houston].”

Claim To Fame L.C. Age

L.C. from Claim To Fame celebrates her birthday on 2 January. She is 33 years old as of July 2022.

Is Claim To Fame L.C. On Instagram?

Yes, Claim To Fame L.C. is on Instagram (@lorealchanelll) and Facebook (@loreal.palmer).

Claim To Fame L.C. Job

According to her Facebook, Claim To Fame L.C. attended Options For Youth San Gabriel and Alan B. Shepard High School. Later on, she attended Pasadena City College and graduated in 2010. She also studied at The Los Angeles Film School.

Loreal helped to write and sing the single “Not in Your Video” for the 2006 film Akeelah and the Bee. She would later write the single “Bottoms Up” for the 2008 film Make It Happen.

According to her IMDB, she worked as a set decorator for Resident Evil: Resurrection. She also has two soundtrack credits Make It Happen and Make It Happen.
