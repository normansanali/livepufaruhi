Missi Pyle Bio, Movies &amp; TV Shows, How Much Is She Worth?

Missi Pyle is a well-rounded Hollywood actress and also a musical artist. She announced her presence in Hollywood after a brilliant performance in the 1999 comedy film Galaxy Quest. She then acted in many films from the year 2000 to 2010, especially in the comedy genre. Some of her notable film credits include 50 First Dates, Charlie and the Chocolate Factory, Soccer Mom, Gone Girl, and Jumanji: Welcome to the Jungle

Pyle has also appeared in numerous television shows and web series over the course of his career, including Jennifer Falls, Frontier Town, and The Man of Souls. The talented actress has become known lately for her starring role as Cleo Coles in the Sci-Fi web series Pulse

From 2007 to 2011, Missi Pyle belonged to the country rock group “Smith & Pyle” which she formed with fellow actress Shawnee Smith. They released an album It’s Good to Be Happy in 2008 and also recorded many notable live performances.

Who is Missi Pyle?

Missi Pyle was born Andrea Kay Pyle on November 16, 1972, to Linda and Frank Pyle. She was born in Houston, Texas but raised in Memphis, Tennessee. She has 4 older siblings and 2 younger half-siblings. She attended Germantown High School in Germantown, Tennessee and for college she was educated at the North Carolina School of the Arts in Winston-Salem, North Carolina.

Movies and TV series

Missi Pyle made her acting debut in 1989 when she played the lead role in a 5th season episode of the Sci-Fi series Star Trek: The Next Generation. She recorded other guest roles on other television shows throughout the 1990s and she also scored her feature film debut on the romantic comedy film As Good As It Gets (1997). She finally got her breakout role on the sci-fi movie Galaxy Quest (1999) in which she played the character of Laliari.

After this brilliant breakout performance, Pyle was cast in numerous comedy films, including Josie and the Pussycats (2001), Knocking Down the House (2003), Along Came Polly (2004), DodgeBall: A Real The Outsider Story (2004), Charlie and the Chocolate Factory (2005) and Just My Luck (2006). She also starred in the action movie Stormbreaker (2006). The talented actress finally landed her first starring role in the independent comedy film Pretty Ugly People (2008) in which she played the character, Lucy.

Missi Pyle also played the lead role of Wendy Handler in the comedy film Soccer Mom (2008) and she also appeared in Miss Nobody (2010), The Artist (2011) and My Uncle Rafael (2012). She also starred in the horror-comedy film A Haunted House 2 (2014) as well as the hit thriller Gone Girl (2014), alongside Ben Affleck.

Other notable film credits of this talented Hollywood star include Uncle Nick (2015), Director’s Blow (2016) as well as the fantasy comedy film Jumanji: Welcome to the Jungle (2017) which was a box office hit. More recently, Missi Pyle appeared in Tyler Perry’s Nobody’s Fooled (2018) and was also cast in the films Ma and Rodeo Hike, both of which are slated for release in 2019.

Pyle’s small-screen credits have been as impressive as his movie credits. She was a main cast member of TV series like The Wedding Bells (2007), Jennifer Falls (2014) and the animated sitcom Border Town (2016). She also starred in the Web Cleansers comedy series (2012-13), Sing! (2016) as well as the hit TV series The Soul Man (2016). In 2018, she was cast as lead character Cleo Coles in the Sci-Fi web series Impulse (2019).

Is Missi Pyle married? Boyfriend

Missi Pyle has been married twice in the past. Between 2000 and 2005, she was married to author and solo performer Antonio Sacre. Three years later, she gave marriage another shot when she tied the knot with wildlife naturalist and TV host Casey Anderson. The duo married on September 12, 2008 in Montana. Their union lasted about 5 years and the actress announced their separation in 2013.

Pyle has also dated actor and comedian Josh Meyers in the past. However, it’s unclear if she’s in a relationship at this time.

How much is it worth?

Although she got off to a rather slow start in her career, Missi Pyle got a major boost in the late 1990s and she has remained relevant ever since. The talented actress may not have featured in too many blockbuster movies, but she’s been very consistent over the years. Through her illustrious acting career and other profitable ventures, the Hollywood star has amassed a net worth valued at an enviable $3 million. Still in her 40s, there doesn’t seem to be any slowing down for Missi Pyle in the near future.
