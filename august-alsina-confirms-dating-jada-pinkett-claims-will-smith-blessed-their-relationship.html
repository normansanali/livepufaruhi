August Alsina Confirms Dating Jada Pinkett, Claims Will Smith Blessed Their Relationship

American singer August Alsina has opened on his relationship with Jada Pinkett, estranged wife of Hollywood actor, Will Smith.

Speaking to Angela Yezee, on the issue for the first time, August Alsina confirmed that he had a romantic affair with Jada Pinket for four years and Will Smith blessed their relationship.

“I don’t like drama. Drama actually makes me nauseous. I also don’t think that it’s ever important for people to know what I do, who I sleep with, who I date, right? But, in this instance, it’s very different, ’cause as I said there is so many people that are side-eyeing me, looking at me questionable about it.
