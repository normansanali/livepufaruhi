Brad Pitt spotted with wedding ring for first time

    Brad Pitt managed to keep his secret marriage to Angelina Jolie under wraps during a photocall on Thursday – the same day that news of his wedding broke. The actor, who wed his belle of nine years over the weekend, slyly kept his left hand in his pocket as he posed for photos at the Bovington Tank Museum in England. Brad had jetted over from Nice, France where his nuptials to Angelina had taken place.

    Brad Pitt attending a photoshoot for new film in England

    After posing with actors Logan Lerman and Jon Bernthal in front of a Sherman Tank, Brad was pictured leaving the museum with his wedding band in clear view.

    THEIR LOVE IN PICTURES TEN YEARS ONThe photoshoot wrapped just before it was confirmed that Brad, 50, and his new wife Angelina, 39, had tied the knot.

    The couple exchanged vows in front of their friends and family, with their six children – Maddox, Pax, Zahara, Shiloh, Vivienne and Knox – all playing a special part. Angelina's eldest sons, Maddox, 12, and Pax, ten, walked her down the aisle while Zahara, nine, and five-year-old Vivienne, threw petals. Shiloh, seven, and Knox, five, served as ring bearers during the non-denominational civil ceremony.

    Brad Pitt married his long-term partner Angelina Jolie in France over the weekend

    Brad and Angelina married at the Chateau Miravel in Correns, the sprawling estate they have been leasing since 2008. The couple got engaged in April 2012, when the actor presented his leading lady with a flawless diamond ring that he had had designed for over a year. The emerald-cut rock is said to have cost £150,000. Ever since, fans have been waiting for the Hollywood supercouple to make it official.
