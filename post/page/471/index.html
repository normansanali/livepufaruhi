A professional cutman for boxing and mixed martial arts matches, Jacob “Stitch” Duran was born on December 29, 1951. Being raised in Planada, California, he is of Mexican origin. A former owner and manager in Fairfield, California, Duran maintained a boxing and kickboxing facility. In addition, he was one of the earliest IKF promoters in California, organizing his first event in October 1992. Prior to pursuing a professional boxing and mixed martial arts career, Duran trained and produced a number of the first IKF Amateur Champions, including Randy “Buzzman” Bussart, Mike “Mohamid” Duran, John “Psyco” Parker, and Andy “The Sandman” Sanchez.
