Early Life and Education

Determined and resourceful, he used his bar mitzvah money to open his first ski shop at just 14 years old.

Sports Team Ownership

Michael G. Rubin’s influence in the sports world extends beyond his successful business ventures. He has also had a notable ownership role in two professional sports teams: the Philadelphia 76ers basketball team and the New Jersey Devils hockey team.

Starting in 2011, Rubin became a partner of the Philadelphia 76ers, a historic NBA franchise based in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. With his business acumen and passion for the game, Rubin played a key role in guiding the team’s operations and growth.

In 2013, Rubin expanded his sports ownership portfolio by becoming a part-owner of the New Jersey Devils, a professional ice hockey team based in Newark, New Jersey. The Devils, known for their rich history and passionate fan base, benefited from Rubin’s strategic insights and business expertise.

However, in June 2022, Rubin made the decision to sell his ownership stakes in both the Philadelphia 76ers and the New Jersey Devils. This choice allowed him to shift his focus and prioritize the expansion of his primary business venture, Fanatics.

Fanatics, the world’s leading provider of licensed sports merchandise, is a major player in the sports retail industry. By selling his ownership stakes, Rubin could dedicate his efforts to further growing and strengthening Fanatics’ position in the market.

Even though Rubin is no longer directly involved in the day-to-day operations of the Philadelphia 76ers and the New Jersey Devils, his influence and contributions to the sports world remain significant.
