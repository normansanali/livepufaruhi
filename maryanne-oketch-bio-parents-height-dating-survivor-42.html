Maryanne Oketch On Survivor 42

A self-proclaimed energetic, empathetic, and chaotic, Maryanne Oketch landed on Survivor 42 believing that she will be able to win the SURVIVOR title with $1 million bucks because she is a social person and the show at its core is a numbers game.

Looking back Maryanne said this one experience in life prepared her for the game.

This was when she had gone for her lifeguarding certificate because of this fear of water left by a nearly-drowning incident when she was a young child. Anyway, Maryanne shared she was the slowest swimmer there. Because she started late, everyone was younger than her and they did well. She on the other hand just failed and failed. Not wanting to give up so easily she kept pushing herself and on her seventh attempt, she finally got the timed swim. This very tenacity is what she said she would be relying on when surviving on the show.

Maryanne Oketch Parents

Survivor 42’s Maryanne Oketch was born to her parents Timothy and Consolata Oketch.

Meet Maryanne’s mother and hero, Consolata Oketch, who has been practicing her profession as a medical doctor since October 2009. Formerly a senior data analyst at Canadian Institute for Health Information, she did her MBChB at the University of Nairobi, MHSc at the University of Toronto and finally she completed her 2-year residency from Queen’s University in 2009.

Talking to CBS for Survivor, Maryanne revealed that her very hardworking mother was born into poverty in a village in Kenya as the youngest of five; she managed to get herself out of poverty and became a doctor in Kenya and provide for her mom; she gave that all up to support Maryanne’s father dreams; became a doctor in Canada while having three young children.

“My mom sacrificed and worked so hard to give her family a better life and never faltered from her dreams. I aspire to be like her. She changed my life”, Maryanne’s mother must be proud hearing her daughter say all this.

Maryanne’s father, Timothy Oketch, on the other hand, is a professional engineer with more than 20 years of working experience and a doctorate, master’s, and bachelor’s in Transporation Engineering.

As per siblings, Maryanne has a sister, Akinyi Oketch, and a brother named Joel Oketch.
