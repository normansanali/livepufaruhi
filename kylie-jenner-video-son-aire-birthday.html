Kylie Jenner's Baby Son, Aire, Is So Cute in These Rare Videos

Happy first birthday to Aire!

Kylie Jenner dedicated a sweet Instagram tribute to her baby boy in celebration of the major milestone. Her video montage includes rare video clips of Aire's first year of life, including shots of Jenner cuddling her son and big sister Stormi playing with her little brother.

"AIRE. my son, my moon, my stars," the Kylie Cosmetics mogul wrote in the caption. "Best year of my life with you. you complete us my angel. mommy loves you. happy 1st birthday. may God always bless you."

Yesterday, Jenner threw a joint birthday bash for her two kids, whom she shares with ex-boyfriend Travis Scott. Stormi turned five years old yesterday (February 1), while Aire celebrated his first birthday today.

She shared a behind-the-scenes look at the luxe birthday party—via her Instagram Story—which included a rainbow bouncy slide, custom-made hoodies and T-shirts, multicolored balloon arches and glittering streamers, and at least two giant silver inflatables of Stormi's face. (The inflatable faces are an homage to dad Scott, who also used giant gold replicas of his head to promote his album Astroworld back in 2018.)

Jenner penned a sweet message for her eldest child yesterday, sharing a slideshow of photos and videos featuring Stormi.

"I gave you the gift of life and life gave me the gift of you," the reality TV star wrote in the post caption. "The most special girl. this little face. i will miss it as it keeps changing. 5 years of loving you and forever more to go. i will always be there for you storm girl."
