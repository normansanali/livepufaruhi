Celebrity Coming Out Stories: Raven-Symone, Dan Levy, More

Sharing with pride! Celebrities like Raven-Symoné, Dan Levy and more have become important figures within the LGBTQIA+ community as they’ve championed equality on and offscreen.

The That’s So Raven alum’s personal life made headlines in August 2013 when she tweeted her reaction to the legalization of same-sex marriage. “I can finally get married! Yay government! So proud of you,” she wrote at the time.

Three years later, Symoné spoke about her journey to self-acceptance and admitted that she “never thought” she would publicly come out.
