Colin Salmon Age, Height, Weight

Colin Salmon Age, Height, Weight: Born on December 6, 1961, Colin Salmon, officially known as Colin Roy Salmon is a British actor.

He developed a passion for acting at a younger age and has been consistent throughout his career rising through the ranks to become one of the most sought-after actors.

Prior to becoming an actor, Salmon had a short stint as a musician. He was a drummer in the punk rock band; The Friction and later worked with another band, the Tee Vee.

He was also a singer and trumpeter. Salmon has his own jazz quartet and played at venues and events including; Dorchester Grill Room and the Cheltenham Jazz Festival.

As an actor, Salmon is best known for playing Charles Robinson in three James Bond films and James “One” Shade in the Resident Evil film series

He has also landed roles in several films and television series including; Doctor Who, Merlin, Arrow, Death In Paradise, Exam, The Musketeers, and Law & Order, to mention a few.

Salmon also played General Zod on the Syfy series Krypton and provided his voice and likeness as Agent Carson in the PlayStation VR video game Blood & Truth.

He partnered with Kristina Rihanoff on the tenth series of BBC One’s Strictly Come Dancing but was eliminated in the fifth week

His other film credits include Captives, Frantz Fanon: Black Skin White Mask, Secret Diary Of A Call Girl, The Wisdom Of Crocodiles, Fanny & Elvis, and My Kingdom.

He played a fictional version of himself in the Netflix series; Master Of None and also portrayed the role of Chudleigh Pomeroy in the films Mortal Engines.

Earlier in 2023, Colin Salmon joined the BBC soap opera EastEnders as George Knight.
