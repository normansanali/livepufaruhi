Maroon 5's Adam Levine Barters With His Motorcycle To Reach No. 1

I always knew Adam Levine would give his fans the shirt off his back — but his prized motorcycle? Guess so. The singer's band, Maroon 5, just released their third album, Hands All Over, on Tuesday — and now he REALLY wants to make sure it reaches No. 1 on next week's Billboard Top 200 albums chart. So much so that he announced on Twitter yesterday that he will give one lucky fan his personal Harley-Davidson Forty-Eight chopper if the disc debuts in the top spot. In a week filled with new releases from JohnLegend and The Roots, Santana, Selena Gomez, the Zac Brown Band and even Liza Minnelli, guess he knows he's got some fierce competition no matter how many raves the Mutt Lange-produced effort is getting.

Want to help the brother out? Visit www.maroon5.com/harley for details, and if Levine sees his dream come true next week, the contest submissions will immediately go live. But that’s not all, folks. Levine is also tossing in a Rider's Edge course to the lucky winner so they can learn and get their official license. Want even more info? Visit maroon5.com and follow Levine at www.twitter.com/adamlevine (Yep, That’s really him!) and start your engines.
