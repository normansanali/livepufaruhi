Overnight Fire Damages Lansdale Restaurant

Crews were dispatched around 1 a.m. to Wimberly Spanish Food Pizza and Pasta at 4th and Walnut Streets in Lansdale, according to Fairmount Fire Company.

There were no injuries reported.

The cause of the fire is under investigation.
