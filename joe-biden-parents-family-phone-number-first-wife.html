Ideologically a moderate member of the Democratic Party, he previously served as the 47th vice president from 2009 to 2017 under President Barack Obama .

He represented Delaware in the United States Senate from 1973 to 2009.

Parents

Joe Biden’s parents were Joseph R. Biden Sr. and Catherine Eugenia “Jean” Finnegan Biden.

They were both born in Pennsylvania and had Irish ancestry.

Joe Biden was the eldest of their four children, followed by his brothers Francis “Frank” and James “Jimmy” and his sister Valerie Biden Owens

Joseph R. Biden Sr. was a businessman who worked as a car salesman, a cleaning-furnace salesman, and a real estate agent.

He faced financial difficulties and moved his family several times between Pennsylvania and Delaware.

He was a Democrat and supported the New Deal policies of Franklin D. Roosevelt.

He died in 2002 at the age of 86.

Catherine Eugenia “Jean” Finnegan Biden was a homemaker who raised her children with love and discipline.

She was a devout Catholic and instilled in her son a sense of faith and service.

Catherine was also a strong-willed and independent woman who encouraged her son to overcome his stutter and pursue his dreams.

She died in 2010 at the age of 92

Family

Joe Biden married his first wife, Neilia Hunter, in 1966.

They met in Nassau, Bahamas, while they were both on spring break.

They had three children: Beau, Hunter, and Naomi.

Neilia was a teacher and played a key role in Joe Biden’s political campaigns.

She died in a car crash in 1972 along with their one-year-old daughter, Naomi.

Their two sons, Beau and Hunter, were seriously injured but survived the incident.

Joe Biden married his second wife, Jill Biden, in 1977.

They met through his brother Frank when Jill was a student at the University of Delaware.

They had one daughter, Ashley, in 1981.

Jill Biden is an educator and a doctor of education.

She has been a supportive partner and a prominent figure in Joe Biden’s political career.

She is the current first lady of the United States.

Joe Biden has seven grandchildren: Natalie and Robert (Beau’s children), Naomi, Finnegan, Maisy, and Beau (Hunter’s children), and Nicholas (Ashley’s son).

He is very close to his grandchildren and often spends time with them.

He has said that they are his “reason to keep going”.

First Wife

Joe Biden’s first wife was Neilia Hunter Biden.

She was born in 1942 in Skaneateles, New York, to Louise and Robert Hunter.

Neilia attended Penn Hall, a boarding school in Pennsylvania, and Syracuse University, where she met Joe Biden.

She worked as an English teacher and served as a campaign manager for Joe Biden.

Tragically, Neilia lost her life in a car crash in 1972, along with their daughter, Naomi.

At the time of her passing, she was 30 years old.

Neilia was not only a loving wife and mother but also a strong supporter of Joe Biden’s political aspirations.

Her contributions were pivotal to his successful U.S. Senate campaign in 1972, where he defeated the incumbent Republican J. Caleb Boggs.

The News Journal described her as the “brains” behind his campaign.

In addition to her political involvement, Neilia was deeply devoted to her children.

She took an active role in their education, teaching them to read and write at a young age.

Neilia, along with her daughter Naomi, rests in peace at St. Joseph on the Brandywine Cemetery in Greenville, Delaware.
