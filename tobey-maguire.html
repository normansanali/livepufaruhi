Tobey Maguire News - Us Weekly

Name:Tobey Maguire

Hometown:Santa Monica, California

Relationship:Divorced

Birthday:June 27, 1975

About

Born in California in 1975, Tobey Maguire was bribed by his mom to take a drama class in high school. Shortly after, he dropped out to pursue acting and landed guest spots on TV series like Blossom and Roseanne.

Soon he was starring in leading roles in blockbusters like 1998’s Pleasantville, the original live-action Spider-Man film series  and 2003’s Seabiscuit.

Once linked to Spider-Man costar Kirsten Dunst, the actor was married to jewelry designer Jennifer Meyer from September 2007 until their divorce was finalized in October 2020, four years after they split. The exes share two children: daughter Ruby, born in 2006, and son Otis, born in 2009.

Maguire moved on with model Tatiana Dieteman. Us Weekly confirmed in March 2020 that the pair were living together, and the model even received Meyer’s stamp of approval.
