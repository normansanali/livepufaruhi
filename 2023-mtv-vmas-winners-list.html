2023 MTV VMAs Winners List

Taylor Swift had more opportunities than any artist to earn trophies at the 2023 MTV Video Music Awards — but she wasn’t the only A-lister who won big.

Stars lined up at the Prudential Center in Newark, New Jersey, on Tuesday, September 12, for the milestone 40th annual awards show. Swift, 33, led the nominations with 11 total, including the coveted Artist of the Year category. She was also up for Video of the Year for her smash hit “Anti-Hero,” breaking her own record with her win. (Swift previously earned three Video of the Year trophies through the years, the most by any artist.)

As fans watched to see who reigned supreme on Tuesday night, they were also treated to a star-studded lineup of performers including Olivia Rodrigo, Kelsea Ballerini and Nicki Minaj, who returned as the ceremony’s emcee for the second year in a row. Shakira and Diddy also took the stage to celebrate their Video Vanguard and Global Icon Awards, respectively.
