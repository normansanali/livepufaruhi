Charlie Wright - Age, Family, Bio

Film and television actor who is best known for his work in the 2017 films Ingrid Goes West and Diary of a Wimpy Kid: The Long Haul.

Before Fame

In 2014, he guest starred on an episode of The Millers.

Trivia

He appeared in a 2018 short film titled The Great American Mud Wrestle. 

Family Life

He was born and raised in Los Angeles, California. 

Associated With

He appeared alongside Aubrey Plaza and Elizabeth Olsen in Ingrid Goes West.
