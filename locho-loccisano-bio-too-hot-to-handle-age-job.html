Locho Loccisano On Too Hot To Handle: Latino

Locho Loccisano was introduced a little later during episode 3, unlike his co-contestants Manuela, Joel, Nico, Carolina, Israel, Zaira, Dadvian, Flor, Lilian, and Nick.

And while Locho seemed to have had his head turned by the lady, Jessica with Clovis were planted in the competition undercover. They were aiming to break the original contestants to break the rules and spend the money. And, Locho as well as the others of course had no clue.

Will Locho manage to leave with any money? Or will his temptations get the better without him? all of these will be revealed no long from now.

So far Locho was doing all he could do to keep his hands off his lady crushes, i.e, of course before Jessica.

Locho Loccisano Family, Nationality

Locho, with the full name Lucas Mariano Loccisano, is from his hometown of Buenos Aires, Argentina. Currently, i.e, in 2021, he lived in Villa Devoto, Buenos Aires, in Argentina. So, his nationality is Argentine.

His father Francisco Loccisano, a Caba, La Union inhabitant has studied Contador Público. While his mother, Monica Meroni, who is from Solís De Mataojo, Lavalleja, Uruguay, went to UBA and Liceo Cuna de Fabini for her studies.

Locho also has a sister named Maria Belen Daniela Loccisano. Maria who is on Twitter @BelenLoccisano is based in Villa Devoto in Buenos Aires, Argentina, and worked as a lawyer at the Ministry of Justice and Human Rights.
