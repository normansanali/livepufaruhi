Dido Bra Size, Age, Weight, Height, Measurements

Biography:

Dido was born on December 25, 1971 in Kensington, London, England. She came to mainstream success after the release of her debut album No Angel in 1999. The album sold over 21 copies internationally and won numerous awards.

In 2003, she released her second album Life for Rent, which also achieved substantial success with the hit singles White Flag and Life for Rent. Her first two albums are among the best-selling albums in UK chart history.

She then went on to release her third album Safe Trip Home in 2008, receiving critical acclaim but was not as commercially successful as its predecessors. She recently released her fourth album, Girl Who Got Away, reaching Top 5 in the UK.
