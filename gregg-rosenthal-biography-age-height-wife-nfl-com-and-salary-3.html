Gregg Rosenthal Biography

Gregg Rosenthal is an American Sports Analyst born and raised in the United States. Currently, he works for NFL Network as an analyst and NFL.com as a writer. He also serves as a Sky Sports analyst and has co-hosted the Around the NFL Podcast for over 1,000 episodes.

    Gregg Rosenthal NFL.com

    Gregg began his career as a sportswriter in 2003. Since 2012, he has worked for the NFL, including as a co-host of the Around the NFL Podcast. He previously worked for NBC Sports, Rotoworld.com, and ProFootballTalk.com, where he covered the NFL for nine years.

    Rosenthal’s most recent covers include running back Adrian Peterson of the Washington Redskins, quarterback Robert Griffin III of the Baltimore Ravens, and tackle Jordan Mailata of the Philadelphia Eagles (2021).
