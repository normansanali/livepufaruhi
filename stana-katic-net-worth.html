Stana Katic’s Rise to Fame

Stana Katic is a Canadian actress with a net worth of $12 million. She attended the Goodman School of Drama in Chicago and began working professionally in the late 90s. Her early film projects included “Acid Freaks”, “Shut-Eye” and “Pit Fighters”.

Katic began to appear in guest roles on multiple TV shows in the early 2000s, including “Alias”, “L.A. Dragnet”, “The Shield”, “JAG”, “The Closer”, “ER”, “24”, “Brothers and Sisters”, “Heroes”, “CSI: Miami”, and “The Unit”. She also worked on films such as “Feast of Love”, “Stiletto”, “Quantum of Solace”, and “The Librarian: Curse of the Judas Chalice”.

Katic’s big break came in 2009 when she was cast as Kate Beckett on the hit series “Castle”. She played the role for eight seasons and became a household name. Today, Katic continues to work in film and TV and is considered one of Canada’s most successful actresses.
