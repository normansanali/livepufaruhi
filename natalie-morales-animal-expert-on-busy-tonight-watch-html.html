Busy Tonight Makes Natalie Moraless Animal-Handling Dreams Come True

Watch the throne, Robert Irwin! There’s a new animal expert in town and she is the best in the biz. Abby’s star Natalie Morales had always wanted to be an animal expert on a talk show, and Busy Philipps gave her the chance. What makes Morales’s animal expert routine so good is that she only has the most cursory of knowledge about any animal. Once she runs out of information fed to her backstage, she has to improvise.

Morales: This is Jasper. He’s a fennec fox. He’s 13-years-old.

Philipps: What’s his deal?

Morales: He’s the smallest of the canines. He’s also a Bernie Bro. He’s from Phoenix, Arizona, and he drives a Dodge Ram. 

You’ll never want to go back to real animal facts again! But her biggest test was when they fed live worms to Monkey (Marcel from Friends).
