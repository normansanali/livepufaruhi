Bella Okagbue - Age, Family, Bio

Nigerian content creator, brand influencer, and reality star who's best known as being a finalist on the reality show Big Brother Naija: Level Up.

Before Fame

She graduated from the University of Lagos.

Trivia

She is the founder of Bella Okagbue Clothing. Her bellaokagbue Instagram account has gained 800,000 followers. 

Family Life

She is in a relationship with Sheggz Olusemo. Her parents are Obi Ofala Okechukwu and Ogechukwu Clara Okagbue. Her brother is Nollywood actor Chris Okagbue. Her sister is actress and model Sandra Okagbue, who is married to Flavour N'abania.

Associated With

She appeared alongside Adekunle Gold on Big Brother Naija: Level Up.
